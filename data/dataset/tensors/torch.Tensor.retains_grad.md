torch.Tensor.retains_grad 
=======================================================================================

Tensor. retains_grad 
:   Is `True`  if this Tensor is non-leaf and its [`grad`](torch.Tensor.grad.html#torch.Tensor.grad "torch.Tensor.grad")  is enabled to be
populated during [`backward()`](torch.Tensor.backward.html#torch.Tensor.backward "torch.Tensor.backward")  , `False`  otherwise.
